React Web App

Love Letters

App that fetches random classic poems that reference the words love and hate from famous poets. Utilizes Poetry API from poetrydb.org

  • Role
  • Dev
  • Year
  • 2021
  • Built With
  • Javascript, React, Redux, Axios

Goal

Creative experiment with async redux

While learning Redux, I wanted to create an interactive app that utilized an interesting API.

Outcome

Love Poem Generator

Love letters gives the user two options, a 'love me' and 'love me not'. Based on the the users choice, the app fetches a classic poem referencing the word 'love' or 'hate' in the format of a love letter.
