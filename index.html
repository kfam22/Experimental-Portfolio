Background

I'm a creative thinker with a knack for problem solving and innovation. My background is in communications, where I studied digital storytelling and the relationship between communication & technology. I have since developed an interest in learning how technology works under the hood. As a software engineer, I enjoy bridging the gap between engineering and UX design — combining my technical knowledge with my eye for design and understanding of communication to create beautiful, easy to use products.

When I'm not in front of a computer screen, I'm probably designing/sewing an elaborate fashion creation, or crossing off another item on my bucket list. more about me
